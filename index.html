About Me

I’m Sudarshan R, an educator with a deep passion for Quantitative Aptitude, Competitive Mathematics, Logical Reasoning, and Data Interpretation. As a faculty member for CAT, IPMAT, GMAT, GRE, and other MBA entrance exams, I specialize in developing and proofreading coaching modules to help students excel in these competitive exams. Additionally, I tutor Science and Mathematics for students in classes 8-12, with a strong focus on preparing for JEE Mains, NEET, and KCET. With an academic background from the prestigious Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru, I hold both MS and BS(Research) degrees, specializing in Sustainability, Life Cycle Assessment, and Environmental Impact. My research expertise continues to inspire my work, as I aim to contribute to a more sustainable world. Beyond education, I’m an author of spiritual and self-help books, and I share my insights through blogging and YouTube videos, particularly on the ancient language of Sanskrit. I am passionate about giving back to the community, which is why I run free educational campaigns and workshops, empowering others to grow and learn.
