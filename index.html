Academic Profile

I am a doctoral researcher at the Centre for Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences, Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru, working on renewable energy systems under climate change risks. My research interests include climate change, renewable energy, sustainability, life cycle assessment, environmental impact, and data-analytical methods.

Alongside research, I have taught quantitative aptitude, data interpretation, logical reasoning, mathematics, and science to diverse learner groups. My teaching experience spans large workshops, one-to-one mentoring, and curriculum-oriented support for competitive examinations and school-level learning.

I also write and create educational content across philosophy, Sanskrit, and interdisciplinary learning. I enjoy translating complex scientific and mathematical ideas into accessible forms through writing, workshops, mentoring, and digital outreach.

Research Experience & Projects

Selected research roles and projects across climate, sustainability, and environmental science.

PhD Research — Renewable Energy Under Climate Risks

Building a research trajectory at the intersection of climate risk, energy transitions, and sustainability-oriented systems analysis at the Centre for Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences, IISc.

Project Associate, DCCC

Conducted literature reviews on sustainable solar expansion and building efficiency, prepared reports and presentations, and assisted methodology development for innovative solar technologies in the Indian economy.

Life Cycle Assessment of Solar Panels

Conducted a comprehensive life cycle assessment of solar panels across production, usage, and end-of-life stages to identify sustainability bottlenecks and propose cleaner energy pathways.

Packed Bed Biofilm Reactor for Efficient Nitrification

Designed and validated a lab-scale packed bed biofilm reactor with 96.6% nitrification efficiency for recirculating aquaculture systems, including mass balance and scalability analysis.

Research Intern, SusPoT — Centre for Sustainability

Worked with a multidisciplinary team on sustainability challenges linked to multiple UN Sustainable Development Goals, supporting data collection and statistical analysis for climate action and energy transition research.

Synthesis of Metal Clusters and IR Analysis

Synthesized polyoxometalate clusters including Mo132 and Mo72Fe30 and performed IR spectral analysis to understand structural-functional relationships in metal cluster chemistry.
