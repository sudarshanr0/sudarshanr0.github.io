Projects

Here are some projects that I have worked on:

  • Project 1: Multi Criteria assessment of EOL Solar PV modules

    – Investigated the environmental impact of solar panels using Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) methodologies, assessing factors such as resource extraction, manufacturing processes, usage, and end-of-life disposal. – Proposed sustainability practices for clean energy by identifying key areas for improvement in solar panel production and disposal.

  • Project 2: Recirculation aquaculture systems(RAS) using Packed bed biofilm reactors(PBR)

    – Evaluated water quality parameters, including total ammonium nitrogen (TAN), nitrite, and nitrate concentrations, and established a mass balance for the system.– Developed and validated methods to improve RAS efficiency with a low bioreactor-to-culture tank volume ratio of 0.042, promoting sustainable aquaculture practices with minimal resource usage. – Proposed scaling solutions for industrial applications of PBR in aquaculture systems to enhance production while reducing environmental impact.

  • Project 3: Synthesis of Metal Clusters and Their IR Analysis

    Synthesized metal-oxo cluster compounds including the polyoxometalate Mo132 Keplerate and Mo72Fe30 clusters, employing advanced chemical techniques and literature-referenced protocols.– Conducted Infrared (IR) spectral analysis to characterize the synthesized compounds, focusing on Mo=O functional group identification and understanding their structural arrangements.
