About Me

I am an educator specialising in quantitative aptitude, competitive mathematics, logical reasoning, and data interpretation for CAT, IPMAT, GMAT, GRE, and other MBA entrance exams. I design and proofread coaching modules that equip students with clear frameworks and exam-ready confidence.

I also tutor science and mathematics for Classes 8–12, with a strong focus on aspirants preparing for JEE Main, NEET, and KCET. My academic roots trace back to the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru, where my research centred on sustainability, life-cycle assessment, and environmental impact.

Outside the classroom and laboratory, I author spiritual and self-development books, blog about Sanskrit and psychology, and host free educational workshops for students and communities across age groups. Empowering others to learn, grow, and lead with purpose drives everything I do.
